EMD Forfeiture Conditions

GTC Clause 4(xiii)(n)
  • Bid withdrawn / modified / impaired within validity
  • Information / documents found false, misleading or forged
  • Failure to furnish Performance Security within stipulated time
  • Multiple bids by same bidder / group (Clause 29) — ALL EMDs forfeited
  • Winner refuses to sign LOA / Contract
GeM SPV may additionally debar: min 3 months first offence, extendable on repeat. (Clause 4(xiii)(o))
